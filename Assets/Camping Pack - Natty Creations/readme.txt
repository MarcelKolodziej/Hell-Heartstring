Thanks for buying the Low Poly Camping pack!
In this pack you will find all the nature and camping items you need to create a modern, outdoor camping scene!
The assets in this pack use a single black/white gradient texture to add a sense to depth and colour variation!

The two included demo scenes will showcase the assets in both Day/Night settings.
